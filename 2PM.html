2PM is one of the 2nd generation K-Pop idols that debuted on September 4, 2008. They used to have seven members, but now, they have only six members, which are Jun. K, Nichkhun, Taecyeon, Wooyoung, Junho and Chansung. Park Jaebeom, now Jay Park, was their 7th member and their leader, but he left the group the year after their debut.

2PM is called beast idols, as they rip their clothes in songs like Heartbeat, I'm Your Man, Back2U, etc.

2PM shows their manly and athletic side through their exposure in Korean entertainment shows, like Running Man due to their physical activities, like name-tag ripping, taking off the target's shoes, etc. Also, their manly side was shown in songs like A.D.T.O.Y, Back2U, Missing U, Good Man, Promise (I'll Be), etc.
